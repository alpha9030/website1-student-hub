          🏆 Practice & Competitive Coding Platforms

          #1

          LeetCode

          Preferred for general software engineering interview prep and algorithmic problem solving.

          • 📅 Daily Challenge: Updates every single day at 00:00 UTC (solve to maintain streaks).
          • 📆 Weekly Contest: Live contest held every Sunday at 02:30 AM UTC.
          • 📆 Biweekly Contest: Live contest held every other Saturday at 14:30 UTC.
          #2

          GeeksforGeeks Practice

          Preferred for company-wise placement sheets and focused DSA preparation.

          • 📅 Problem of the Day (POTD): New problem released every day (great for consistent prep).
          • 📆 Weekly Contest: Live placement contest held every Sunday at 13:30 UTC / 7:00 PM IST.
          #3

          HackerRank

          Preferred for beginner syntax mastery, learning fundamentals, and domain badges.

          • 📅 Flexible Challenges: Includes structured 1-Week and 1-Month Interview Preparation Kits.
          #4

          CodeChef

          Preferred for beginner-friendly competitive coding and division-based learning.

          • 📅 CodeChef Starters: Live contest held every Wednesday at 14:30 UTC (Weekly).
          • 📆 Daily Practice: Handpicked daily practice challenges for constant learning.
          #5

          Codeforces

          Preferred for high-level competitive programming and international rating updates.

          • 📅 Regular Rounds: Live contests held 2-3 times per week (Div. 1-4) at various times.
          • 📆 Daily Practice: Archive database updates daily with community-rated problems.
          #6

          AtCoder

          Preferred for algorithm-heavy and mathematical contests based in Japan.

          • 📅 AtCoder Beginner Contest (ABC): Live weekly contest held every Saturday at 12:00 UTC.
          • 📆 AtCoder Regular Contest (ARC): Held on alternating Sundays.
          #7

          Codewars

          Preferred for interactive rank-up exercises (katas) in a community-driven sandbox.

          • 📅 Daily Kata: Rank-up challenges updated daily by the developer community.
          #8

          Exercism

          Preferred for mentor-led language practice and local CLI workflow training.

          • 📅 Self-Paced Tracks: Complete tasks at any time with active human mentor reviews.
          #9

          Project Euler

          Preferred for math-heavy algorithms, number theory, and logic programming challenges.

          • 📅 Mathematical Challenges: Archives of 800+ logic-focused programming problems.
          #10

          Kaggle

          Preferred for data science competitions, machine learning models, and datasets analysis.

          • 📅 Active Competitions: Live predictive challenges with global rank badges and leaderboards.
